Poems
Kala
by K Natarajan

Kerala the best of lands
Amongst all and sundry
Lord thanks to thee I always
Am proud to be Keralite
  
Kathakali, Kaikottikkali and more
Ambalam, Adappayasam and Arts
Literature leads the world
Always Malayalam on top.

  
Kala is never complete
A la carte it does potray
Literature if one looks
A la mode one could say.

  
Keep in touch galore
Aims this groups no more
Like-minded people meet
Aspire, articulate and act

  
Kneading the young minds
Attract talents latent
Loiter if prevent this can
Achieved KALA has something.
