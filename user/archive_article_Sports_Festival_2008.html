KALA Sports Festival 2008

KALA members got together once again with family and friends at the Ferry Sports Centre in Oxford on 29th November 2008 to enjoy an afternoon of activity and games. There was a good turn out with people both ready to compete and cheer the competitors. The tournaments in badminton for men and women were followed by friendly basketball and 5-a-side football matches, which got most of the crowd taking part and enjoying the competition. The younger kids participated in events such as lime and spoon race and sack race and also found enjoyment releasing their energy on a bouncy castle.

The sport that probably highlighted everyone’s competitive streak and set everyone’s adrenalin pumping was separate men’s and women’s tug of war competitions. The activities and games were splendidly organised by Dr Jose Mathews and Dr. Venugopal and at the end of the four hours of physical exertions, everyone was ready for food and drink. Nutritious replenishment was afforded in the form of mouth watering prawn and chicken starters by Jose and Simmy and this was complemented with a wide variety of delicious dishes by the wonderful ladies of KALA.
