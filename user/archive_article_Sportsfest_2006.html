KALA - Sportsfest 2006

This year’s KALA Youth sports Day was held at Farnborough Leisure Centre on 16th December 2006. After last years’ success KALA youth requested Dr.Jose Mathews to organise once again this year! Dr.Jose did it even better this time! More than 85 members and friends took part in the event. It was an opportunity for KALA members to show their athletic and sporting talent. There was Badminton, Football, Table Tennis and Dodgy ball game for women. The younger children enjoyed the game of “Lemon & Spoon Race” and of course, the bouncy castle which was enjoyed both by the young and old! There were light refreshments and drinks during break. The activities came to a close at 7.00 P.M. After the enjoyable day everyone gathered at Drs. Sukumaran & Sethulekshmi’s house for a sumptuous Dinner. Sethu made sure all the hungry and tired participants were fed. All the food items were very tasty and the star item being home cooked “Pizza” with different toppings made by Dr.Jose himself with fresh ingredients! Well done to Dr.Jopse’s culinary expertise! A perfect end to an exciting days’ event. The KALA youth are already looking forward to next Sports Day!
